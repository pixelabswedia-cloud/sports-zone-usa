# NFL Draft 2026: Biggest Winners and Losers from Round 1

The first round of the 2026 NFL Draft is officially in the books. From surprise trades to unexpected quarterback slides, last night was a rollercoaster for football fans. 

Here is our analysis of who came out on top and who left fans scratching their heads.

### The Winners:
* **Chicago Bears:** Once again, the Bears managed to secure elite protection for their franchise QB. Their draft board management was flawless.
* **New York Jets:** They focused on defensive depth, picking up the best cornerback available in the late first round.

### The Losers:
* **Dallas Cowboys:** A questionable reach for an offensive tackle that many analysts projected as a second-rounder. 
* **Fantasy Managers:** With many top RBs going to teams with established starters, the "rookie hype" for fantasy might be lower than expected this year.
